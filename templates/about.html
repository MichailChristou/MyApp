About Us

Welcome to our Energy Efficiency Prediction Web App! Our mission is to help you optimize your home's energy consumption using cutting-edge machine learning techniques. By analyzing building data and energy performance, our app provides personalized recommendations to improve your home's energy rating.

Our team consists of data scientists and software engineers passionate about sustainable living and environmental impact reduction. We strive to make energy efficiency accessible and easy to understand for everyone.

We believe that small changes can lead to significant savings — both for your wallet and for the planet. Start using our app today and make your home more energy efficient!
